## Methodology

- **category_accuracy** - Predicted category matches the expected label.
- **priority_accuracy** - Predicted priority matches the expected label.
- **citation_recall** - When a KB document is expected, at least one retrieved chunk comes from it.
- **required_phrase_coverage** - At least one of the 3 drafts contains one of the must_contain_any phrases.
- **prohibited_phrase_violations** - Total count of must_not_contain phrases that leaked into drafts. Must be 0.
- **faithfulness_proxy** - Fraction of drafts that cite at least one [doc-N] when retrievals exist. Replaced by LLM-judge in live mode.
- **latency_p95_ms** - p95 of end-to-end pipeline latency.
- **cost_per_ticket_usd** - Mean cost per ticket across all drafts.

In live mode, faithfulness is scored by an LLM judge against
the retrieved chunks. In mock mode it falls back to a proxy:
fraction of drafts that include at least one `[doc-N]` citation
when retrievals are available.
