# Refund Policy

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. Refunds must be requested within 14 calendar days of the original charge.

## How to request a refund

Customers can request a refund by emailing billing@example.com or by opening a ticket from their account dashboard. Include the order ID and the reason for the refund request.

## Processing times

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The money is returned to the original payment method. For credit cards, the refund typically appears on the customer's statement within 5-10 business days, depending on the issuing bank.

## What is and is not refundable

Refundable:
- Annual subscription within the first 14 days
- Accidental duplicate charges
- Charges for plans the customer never used (no logins after purchase)

Not refundable:
- Charges older than 14 days
- Add-on features (SMS credits, AI tokens) that have already been consumed
- Custom enterprise contracts (refund terms specified in the signed agreement)

## Enterprise contracts

Enterprise customers with annual commitments follow the refund terms in their signed MSA. Standard 14-day policy does not apply.

## Escalation

If a customer is dissatisfied with the standard refund response, escalate to the billing supervisor (billing-leads@example.com). Mention "billing escalation" in the subject line.
